My favorite dessert, Leche Flan.

Leche Flan is, without a doubt, my absolute favorite dessert. Every bite feels like a sweet embrace—smooth, creamy, and utterly indulgent. The rich caramel on top adds a perfect balance of sweetness, creating an irresistible combination that melts in your mouth. What I love most about Leche Flan is its delicate texture. It’s soft yet firm, a custard so silky it seems to glide across your tongue.

Each time I take a spoonful, I’m reminded of warmth and comfort, as though it’s a treat meant to be savored slowly, without any rush. There's something magical about how the flavors unfold as you eat it—rich, velvety, and comforting, it has a way of filling you with pure happiness. Whether it’s served during a celebration or as an everyday indulgence, Leche Flan will always hold a special place in my heart.
